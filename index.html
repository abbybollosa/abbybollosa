30-Day Physical Activity Program & Meal Planning

30-Day Physical Activity Program

This plan includes strength training, cardio, and flexibility exercises suitable for all fitness levels.

Day 1-10: Beginner

Strength Training: 15-minute bodyweight exercises like squats, lunges, and push-ups

Cardio: 20-30 minutes of walking, jogging, or cycling

Flexibility: 10-minute stretching routine

Day 11-20: Intermediate

Strength Training: 20-30 minutes with dumbbells or resistance bands

Cardio: 30 minutes of brisk walking or running

Flexibility: 15 minutes of yoga or stretching

Day 21-30: Advanced

Strength Training: 30-45 minutes focusing on weightlifting or resistance

Cardio: 45 minutes of high-intensity interval training (HIIT)

Flexibility: 20-minute advanced yoga or Pilates
